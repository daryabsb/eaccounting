

Steps:
    1. Create a new company (or open existing).
    2. Company creation instructions and steps.
    3. Choose peachtree product:
        a. Accounting Program
        b. -- For construction.
        c. -- For destribution.
        d. -- For manufacturing.
        e. -- For non-profits.
        f. -- Normal app.
    4. Enter company information.
    5. Select method that matches your company.
    6. Choose the template.
    7. Accounting method (Acrual or Cash).
    8. Posting method (Real time recording or Batch recording of transactions).
    9. Accounting period structor (12 monthly or custom calendar).
    10. First period physical calendar (Month, Year).
    11. Completion message (Finish)
